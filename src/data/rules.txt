**1. Follow Discord’s Code of Conduct.** These rules can be found here: <https://discord.com/guidelines>

**2. Respect others and remain civil.** Refrain from trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks. We also do not tolerate harassment or publishing others’ private information (including physical or electronic addresses, pictures, names) without explicit permission. Please do not post screenshots from Zoom with names or faces. 

**3. Keep posts and content safe for work.** We do not allow the use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances. We also do not allow overly sexual content even if it isn't aimed at others. We also do not allow references to suicide and self-harm, even through memes.

**4. Illegal content is not permitted.** We do not allow spreading of unlawful content, such as public links to textbook downloads. Do not ask where to find textbooks or other illegal content. We also do not allow conversations about controlled substances or drugs. 

**5. Registered and active student orgs are allowed to advertise in #orgs-and-events⁠. ** To advertise your student organization, talk to a moderator and we will give a role that allows posting in the channel. 

**6. Please do not post grades or GPA.** Posting grades and GPA can lead to people comparing themselves to others in unhealthy ways so we think it is best to not permit it. Please share grades privately if you feel the need to. 

**Message Logging**
Any messages that are edited or deleted are logged into a private channel on the server. This is done to ensure that abuse of our rules isn't being hidden from moderators.
